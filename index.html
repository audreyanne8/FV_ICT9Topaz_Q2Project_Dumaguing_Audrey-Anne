Lutong Visayan

Lutong Visayan is a website created to showcase the delicious Visayan Cuisine.
Visayas is a land known for its rich and flavorful food. In this website, you'll be able to learn many Visayan dishes and even submit your own! Mangaon ta!

The Visayan Regions

Visayas is split into 4 different regions. Namely, Western Visayas, Eastern Visayas, Negros Island Region, and Central Visayas.
Western Visayas: Known for being the home to world-famous beaches, famous festivals, and flavorful dishes like La Paz Batchoy.
Eastern Visayas: Has many historical landmarks, islands like Kalanggaman, and famous dishes like Binagol and Moron
Negros Island Region: Known for its scenic sugarcane & delicacies like Piaya and Kansi.
Central Visayas Renowned for its beaches in Cebu and Bohol, iconic Cebu Lechon, and tourist attractions like the Chocolate Hills.
