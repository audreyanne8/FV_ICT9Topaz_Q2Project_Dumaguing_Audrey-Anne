Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef shanks
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • cups of water
  • annatoto powder
  • tamarind powder soup base
  • green jackfruit, 8 pieces kamias, chili
  • sinigang mix, 2 potato leaes, and salt&pepper
<

Instructions

  1. Rinse beef shanks well to remove any blood or impurities. Drain well.
  2. Peel the outer layer of the lemongrass stalks until you reach the pale central core. Cut into 3 to 4-inch lengths and lightly pound each piece with the back of a knife. Set aside.
  3. In a deep pot over medium heat, heat oil. Add the beef shanks and cook, turning as needed, until lightly seared.
  4. Add garlic and cook, stirring regularly, until softened.
  5. Add water and bring to a boil, skimming any scum that floats on top. When the broth clears, add the onion.
  6. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for about 1 ¬Ω to 2 hours or until the meat is fork-tender.
  7. In a small bowl, combine annatto powder with about ¬Ω cup of the broth. Stir until the annatto powder is dissolved and return the broth to the pot.
  8. Add the tamarind soup base. Stir to disperse.
  9. Add lemongrass, green jackfruit, kamias, and chili peppers. Continue to cook for about 10 to 15 minutes or until the jackfruit pieces are heated.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
